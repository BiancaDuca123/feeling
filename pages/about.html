About

Welcome to the Feelings Project, where we celebrate the beauty and complexity of human emotions. Our mission is to create a space for exploration, understanding, and appreciation of the feelings that connect us all.

Our Mission

At Feelings Project, we aim to inspire mindfulness and compassion through stories, insights, and reflections on emotions. We believe in fostering deeper connections between people by understanding the shared experiences that make us human.

Our Vision

We envision a world where empathy and self-awareness lead to stronger relationships and personal growth. By embracing the full spectrum of emotions, we hope to build a community that uplifts and supports one another.

The Team

Founded by Bianca-Ecaterina Duca, the Feelings Project brings together a passionate team of storytellers, designers, and thinkers who share a commitment to emotional well-being and artistic expression.

Join Us

We invite you to explore our content, share your stories, and connect with us as we embark on this journey of emotional discovery together. Let's celebrate the feelings that make life beautiful.
